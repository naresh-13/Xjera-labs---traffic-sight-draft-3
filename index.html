About Us



Established in 2013, XJERA LABS is a Singapore based emerging market leader of Artificial Intelligence and video analytics solutions in Singapore and Asia.

We have received recognition by numerous government agencies in Singapore. Our AI video analytics have attained first place for the following challenges,

  • 2015, the Infocomm Development Authority (IDA) Inaugural Video Analytics Challenge.
  • 2017, Singapore Airport Video Analytics Challenge.
  • 2019, Emerging Enterprise Award jointly organized by The Business Times and OCBC.


These, as well as winning other iconic projects, has marked XJERA LABS as a highly accredited company in the smart city domain.

Our Clients



Traffic-Sight™

TrafficSight™ provides users an opportunity to leverage of our video analytics solution, using their own modern-day cameras, to conduct highly accurate traffic surveys.

With the revolutionary deep learning framework developed by Xjera labs, TrafficSightTM is a robust solution which can perform under challenging environment, such as reflection shadows, shaky solution, night condition and low resolution yet still achieving a consistent performance above 90%*.

* Achieving a consistent performance above 90%. Performance of 70ms in response time with over 90% accuracy based on testing with NVIDIA advanced GPU
